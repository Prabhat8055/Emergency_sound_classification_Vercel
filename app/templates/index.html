About the Project

Our Emergency Sound Classification Project is designed to identify critical sounds such as dog barks, gunshots, glass breaks, baby cries, and sirens, which often signal emergencies or potential dangers. By leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, the system processes audio input, classifies it into one of six predefined categories, and quickly determines whether the situation requires attention. Once a sound is detected and classified, the system ensures immediate action by automatically sending an alert email via Gmail to relevant individuals or authorities. This functionality enhances response times in emergencies and increases overall situational awareness, making it an essential tool for personal safety, security, and monitoring. This project highlights the seamless integration of sound detection, classification, and real-time notification, showcasing the potential of AI to create practical and life-saving applications. The system is particularly useful for scenarios like smart homes, public spaces, and industrial environments where rapid response to critical events is crucial.
